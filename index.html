Our Mission

A JENuine VISion for sophomore year.

At Stuyvesant High School, students can start to feel as if they’re going through a constant cycle of commuting, assignments, studying, and exams. This in turn burns them out a lot, since students focus more on surviving through the week. Our campaign is focused on transitioning into a more balanced, unified student life, making sure to keep both social and academic spirit alive.

What We Plan To Do

Supporting both student wellness and academic success.

🎉 Events & Community

We want to give sophomores something to look forward to post-big exams, so that students can decompress and build community. This can include big events like “are you smarter than a fifth grader nights?”, or playoff like sports competitions/field days (can take place inside the school or on the park outside).

✨ Wellness Improvements

Along with this, we want to create small improvements that will improve the sophomore experience. Practical little carts could be packed with tools that students could use to de-stress. We could also implement more accessible wellness supplies, such as first aid kits. Many students also pack food from home; therefore, supervised microwaves are also an improvement we are looking forward to (most likely either by the sophomore bar or the cafeteria).

📚 Academic Support

We also want to improve student academic success by implementing feedback systems. This will be done by anonymous scheduled check-ins for both students and teachers to get an idea of workload, resources, opportunities, and experience, and then allow them to fairly accommodate.

💕 Student Social Media Platform

Finally, a unified community is what will truly bring the sophomore student body together. We will build a student-run social media platform that reflects the sophomore experience and voices, with rotating members in charge of it so they don’t have to have all the work put on them. This page will include fun, relatable trends (like so called ‘music videos’ that give details of the month to come), as well as quick volunteer opportunities, study help, advice, and personality quizzes to help get to know the grade better!
