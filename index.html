Running for Sophomore Caucus 2026

Our Mission

At Stuyvesant High School, students can begin to feel trapped in a constant cycle of commuting, assignments, studying, and exams. Our campaign focuses on creating a more balanced and unified sophomore experience where both academic success and student life matter.

Events & Community 💕

We want sophomores to have exciting events to look forward to after stressful exams and heavy workloads. Students deserve opportunities to decompress, have fun, and connect with each other.

  • “Are You Smarter Than a Fifth Grader?” nights
  • Sports playoffs & field days
  • School competitions and games
  • Community-building activities

Wellness Improvements ✨

Small changes can make a huge difference in student life. We want to introduce practical improvements that support student wellness and reduce stress throughout the school day.

  • Stress-relief carts
  • Accessible wellness supplies
  • More first aid kits
  • Supervised microwaves near the sophomore bar or cafeteria

Academic Support 📚

Academic success should come with support and communication. We want students and teachers to better understand workload, stress levels, and classroom experiences.

  • Anonymous student check-ins
  • Teacher feedback systems
  • Fairer accommodations
  • Improved communication between students and staff

Student Social Media Platform 🎀

We want to create a student-run social media platform that represents the sophomore experience and keeps students informed, connected, and involved.

  • Monthly “music video” updates
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Study help and advice
  • Fun trends and relatable content
  • Personality quizzes and grade interaction
